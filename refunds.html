Important Notice Before Purchase

Affiliate Marketing Mastery offers digital educational products. Upon successful payment, users receive immediate access to the course content. Because the product is delivered digitally, refunds are generally not available except under specific circumstances described below. We encourage all users to review the course details before purchasing.

1. Nature of the Product

Affiliate Marketing Mastery provides a digital online course delivered through video lessons and training materials. The course is delivered electronically and becomes accessible immediately after payment confirmation.

2. Refund Eligibility (7-Day Window)

We provide a 7-day satisfaction window. Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the original purchase date to be considered. Requests made after this period will not be eligible for a refund.

3. Eligible Refund Situations

Refunds are only processed in the following verified cases:

  • Duplicate Payment: If you were charged twice for the same account due to a technical error.
  • Technical Failure: If you are unable to access the course content due to a platform-side technical error that our support team cannot fix within 72 hours.
  • Unauthorized Transaction: Verified cases of fraudulent use of your payment method (requires a bank/police report).

4. Invalid Refund Situations (Non-Refundable)

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Change of mind or personal preference after accessing the course.
  • Inability to dedicate time to the training.
  • Failure to achieve specific financial results (as outcomes depend on individual effort).
  • Partial completion of the course content.
  • Violation of our Terms and Conditions leading to account suspension.

4. Refund Request Procedure

If you qualify for a refund under the above conditions, you must submit a request within 7 days of the purchase date.

If a refund request is approved:
